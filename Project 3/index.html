The fourth generation we all know and love

Why We Love Them


GM's fourth generation F-body was around for a short, but exciting 9 years! They gave us two continuations of their well-known muscle car line: Camaro and Firebird. With two very fun and roudy corvette engines introduced, these cars were one of the best horsepower-for-your-buck cars out to compete!

What They Offered Us


While GM has always cattered to the budget-friendly consumer and offer base packages for those who appreciate the aggressive style of the fourth gen F-body, they provided the sport trims that everyone craves and more.


Camaro

Offered in two sport trims famously known as, Z28 (Pictured Left) and SS (Pictured Right).


Firebird

Offered in four sport trims known as, Formula, Trans Am(T/A), WS6, and Firehawk
